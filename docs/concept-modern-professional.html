Meet Your Guide

Your Journey to Mastery

Flow state isn't just a buzzword—it's that perfect moment when technique, timing, and intuition align on the water.

Hi, I'm Tanner. After 12 years of guiding anglers through New Mexico's diverse waters, I've learned that great fly fishing isn't just about perfect casts—it's about understanding the water, reading the conditions, and finding your personal rhythm.

My approach combines technical precision with mindful practice. Whether you're brand new to fly fishing or looking to elevate your skills, I'll help you develop confidence, improve your technique, and most importantly, have an incredible time on the water.

From the high mountain streams to the legendary waters of the San Juan, every session is tailored to your goals and skill level. Together, we'll work on everything from basic casting mechanics to advanced presentation techniques, all while enjoying some of the most beautiful fishing destinations in the Southwest.
